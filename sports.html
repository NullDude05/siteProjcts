Sports

Basketball, Baseball, Football, and Swimming. My favorite sports
would be Basketball and Baseball. The sport that I enjoy the
most would be Basketball. Basketball is a fun sport to me
because it is very competitive and it tests your skills. I like baseball
because I like the feeling of pressure when you have to run as fast
as you can so that you can get to base before they catch you.
The other sports are entertaining and fun when you play with friends or with other people.
