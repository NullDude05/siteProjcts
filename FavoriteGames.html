Favorite Games

My most favorite game of all time (for now) is "The Last of Us". The game is about a zombie apocalypse and you have to take care of a girl while trying to survive the outbreak. As you progress through the game you get attatched to the girl as your own daughter. Overall, I love playing games that have a good story and have an interesting playing technique.
